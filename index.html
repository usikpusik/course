Hello Neighbor

Hello Neighbor is a Stealth Horror Game about sneaking into your neighbor’s house and figuring out what he’s hiding in the basement. Play against an advanced AI that learns from your actions

Hello Neighbor is provided via Steam key for Windows. For key redemption, a free Steam account is required

Feature

  • Suspenseful horror gameplay (not jump scares) that focuses on sneaking around your neighbor’s house
  • Constantly evolving experience The Neighbor’s AI counters your moves and learns from what you do
  • Sandbox-style gameplay Plenty of environmental interaction and physics
